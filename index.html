Goods Import & Export

We could help you identify reliable suppliers from China that meet your quality and budget requirements.
We could also guide you through the regulations of import & export laws and tariffs to ensure your operations are fully compliant.
Besides, we provide on-site inspections or manage third-party audits to ensure that your products meet all quality standards.
In addition, our marketing research team can help identify promising markets for your products.
Finally, our experts will prepare all necessary documents to make the export process as smooth as possible.

International Payments

Our partnerships with financial institutions will provide you with the funding solutions you need to grow your export business.
We have professional operations to handle currency conversion, currency hedging and payments associated with international trade.
Besides, we provide a legal and efficient way for exporters to reclaim some or all of the domestic taxes they have paid.
This is typically facilitated through a streamlined electronic process to reduce paperwork and expedite refunds.
With the inclusion of export tax rebate services, international payment platforms not only simplify cross-border transactions but also provide an additional financial advantage for exporters.

Logistics & Customs Clearance

We manage and organize the transportation of goods, giving your best choice among seashipping, air transport, rail, and road transport. We assist clients with all customs-related procedures associated with the import and export of goods.
From shipping to customs clearance, we've got it all covered. Your goods will be safely transported from the supplier to your warehouse.
With our network of international shippers, your products will reach their destination on time and in perfect condition.

About Us

CoTeX Import & Export Co., Ltd. (CoTeX Co., Ltd. below) is a recognized textile-related trade company. Established in 2013, we are dedicated to promoting the growth and advancement of the textile industry worldwide.


Core Business Offerings:

Raw Materials: We source the finest quality raw materials essential for textile production, ensuring our clients receive the best in terms of quality and cost-efficiency.


Production Equipment: Our catalog boasts a wide range of cutting-edge machinery and tools, tailored to cater to the evolving needs of the textile manufacturing sector.


Auxiliary Materials: We provide a comprehensive selection of auxiliary materials, crucial for enhancing the production process and the quality of the finished products.


Finished Products: Our extensive network allows us to export a diverse range of high-quality textile products, ensuring our partners have access to the best the industry has to offer.
